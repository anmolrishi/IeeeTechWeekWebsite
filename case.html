IEEE NSIT Case Challenge

IEEE NSIT presents a case study which would revolve around a crisis faced by a tech-startup. The premise of the case is pivoted around recent happenings in the financial world or on major technological advancements like AI, machine learning etc. A strategic collaboration with a tech-startup would provide students with real-time insight of the technical challenges faced by these startups and a chance to challenge their technical know how.
