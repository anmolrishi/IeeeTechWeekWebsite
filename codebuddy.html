Codebuddy

Online round with 20-25 questions ranging from analog to digital would be required to be answered within 45-60 minutes. Top seven teams shortlisted would be further given problem statement, the teams would then solve the problem and realise the circuit with all the components being used with their exact values. The simulation would be done based on the realised circuit on software like PSpice/Altium /Proteus. Top three team would then work upon circuit realisation using hardware like breadboard and other components.
